## Summary

Blockiverse VR is a single- and multi-player voxel building game for Meta Quest. The game is
designed to collect as little personal data as possible. It does not include advertising, and
it does not sell personal data.

## Information we collect

- **Account / platform data:** When you use Meta platform features (such as Meta Horizon
  avatars or party chat), that functionality is provided by Meta under Meta's own privacy
  policy. Blockiverse VR receives only the minimum identifiers required to display avatars and
  connect players in a session.
- **Local game data:** World saves, player inventory, and comfort/settings preferences are
  stored locally on your device. This data stays on the device unless you choose to share a
  save file yourself.
- **Multiplayer connection data:** In LAN multiplayer, devices exchange IP addresses and game
  state directly to host or join a session. This information is used only to maintain the
  active session and is not stored after the session ends.
- **Diagnostic logs:** The game writes local diagnostic logs to help debug crashes and
  performance issues. Logs are stored on the device and are not transmitted automatically.

## Information we do not collect

- We do not collect or transmit precise location data.
- We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
- We do not sell or rent personal data to third parties.

## Voice chat

Blockiverse VR does not include in-app voice chat. Voice communication between players uses
Meta Quest party chat, which is governed by Meta's privacy policy.

## Children's privacy

Blockiverse VR is not directed to children under the age required by Meta's platform policies
in your region. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Data sharing and third parties

The only third-party platform integrated is Meta (Horizon avatars, party chat, and store
services). Their handling of data is governed by Meta's privacy policy.

## Your choices

- You can delete local game data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.
- You can disable Meta platform features through your Meta account settings.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the game evolves. Material changes will be reflected by the
"Last updated" date above and in the published policy URL.
